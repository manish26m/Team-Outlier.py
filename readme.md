<h2 id="features">Features</h2>
<h3>Health Tips and Related News</h3>
<p>Stay informed with daily or weekly updates on health tips and the latest news. Our curated content keeps you up-to-date and motivated toward a healthier lifestyle.</p>
<h3>Doctor Appointment Scheduler</h3>
<p>Effortlessly schedule appointments with qualified doctors based on your symptoms and potential health concerns. Our smart scheduling system connects you with healthcare professionals efficiently.</p>
<h3>Medication Reminder</h3>
<p>Ensure you never miss a dose with our medication reminder system. Timely alerts help you stay on track with your prescribed medications.</p>
<h3>Health Predictor</h3>
<p>Access your symptom check history and receive predictions on potential health conditions. Our Health Predictor uses advanced algorithms to help you anticipate and understand your health needs.</p>
<h3>Chatbot Assistance</h3>
<p>Meet your virtual health assistant! Our enhanced chatbot helps with booking appointments, providing health tips, and guiding you through simple exercises for improved wellness.</p>
<h3>Community Support</h3>
<p>Connect with others who have similar health experiences. Our Community Support feature offers a space for users to share stories, exchange advice, and find encouragement.</p>
<h3>Nutrition and Diet Planner</h3>
<h4>Diet Suggestions</h4>
<p>Receive personalized diet plans tailored to your health conditions, recovery progress, or fitness goals.</p>
<h4>AI Integration</h4>
<p>Enjoy AI-driven meal and recipe recommendations that align with your specific health needs, making healthy eating easier and more enjoyable.</p>
<h3>Emergency Contact Feature</h3>
<p>Instantly contact emergency contacts or local emergency services when you need urgent help.</p>
<h3>Health Risk Assessment</h3>
<p>Evaluate your risk for various diseases based on your lifestyle, habits, and family history.</p>
<h3>Superior User Experience and Resource Reliability</h3>
<h4>User Onboarding</h4>
<p>New to MEDILINK? Our guided tour introduces you to all features and functionalities, making it easy to get started and maximize the platform.</p>

<h2 id="installation">Installation</h2>
<ol>
<li><strong>Clone the repository:</strong></li>
<code class="block">git clone https://github.com/yourusername/medilink.git</code>
<li><strong>Navigate to the project directory:</strong></li>
<code class="block">cd medilink</code>
<li><strong>Install backend dependencies:</strong></li>
<code class="block">pip install -r backend/requirements.txt</code>
<li><strong>Install frontend dependencies:</strong></li>
<code class="block">cd frontend<br>npm install</code>
<li><strong>Run the development server:</strong></li>
<code class="block">npm start</code>
<li><strong>Start the backend server:</strong></li>
<code class="block">cd ../backend<br>python app.py</code>
</ol>
